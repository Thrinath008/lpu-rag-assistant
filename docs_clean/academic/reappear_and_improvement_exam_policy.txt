TITLE LPU Reappear and Improvement Examination Policy

OVERVIEW Lovely Professional University (LPU) provides students with the opportunity to appear in Reappear Examinations to clear failed courses and in Improvement Examinations to upgrade grades in passed courses. Both facilities are accessible through the UMS or the Re-appear and Improvement portal. This policy outlines the eligibility, process, number of attempts, and the effect of these examinations on the student's academic record.

KEY RULES AND POLICY DETAILS

Reappear Examinations (Backlog Clearance)

A student who fails a subject or scores below the minimum passing threshold is eligible to apply for a Reappear Examination for that course.

Reappear examinations are generally held at the end of each semester or during the summer term, as per the official academic calendar and datesheet published by the university.

A student is generally allowed up to 5 attempts to clear a failed subject.

Failure to clear a subject within the permitted number of attempts may affect the student's promotion to subsequent semesters or the award of the degree.

Improvement Examinations (Grade Upgrade)

Both current students and passed-out students are eligible to apply for Improvement Examinations to upgrade their grade in a subject they have already passed.

The Improvement Examination is conducted in the same format as a regular End-Term Examination.

The better of the two marks — the original marks or the improvement examination marks — is retained in the student's final academic record.

If a subject is not listed or available in the Improvement window on the portal, the student may submit a written application to the Examination Department requesting a special improvement opportunity, after consulting their Dean or Examination Cell staff.

EFFECT ON ACADEMIC RECORDS

Reappear or Improvement Examinations are not indicated or mentioned on the student's final marksheet or degree certificate. Only the final grade or marks achieved in the subject are reflected.

However, pending reappear or improvement examinations may delay the completion of academic formalities and the release of the final degree certificate, as all pending examination records must be cleared before the degree is issued.

PROCEDURE — HOW TO APPLY

For Reappear Examinations

Log in to UMS or the Re-appear and Improvement portal.

Check the list of courses available for reappear in the current examination window.

Apply for the relevant course(s) and pay the applicable examination fee.

Appear for the examination as per the official datesheet.

For Improvement Examinations

Log in to UMS or the Re-appear and Improvement portal.

Check whether the subject is listed in the Improvement window.

If listed, apply for the subject and pay the applicable fee.

If the subject is not listed, submit a written application to the Examination Department, after consulting the Dean or Examination Cell staff, requesting a special improvement chance.

Appear for the examination as per the official datesheet.

IMPORTANT CONDITIONS

The better of the original and improvement examination marks is always retained; improvement examination marks cannot reduce a previously obtained grade.

Pending backlogs or improvement examinations can delay degree issuance. Students are advised to clear all pending examinations promptly.

The university's official academic calendar must be consulted for the schedule of reappear and improvement examination windows.

Reappear and improvement statuses are not disclosed on the final degree or marksheet.

CONTACT AND RESPONSIBLE OFFICE

Division of Examinations: Responsible for scheduling and conducting reappear and improvement examinations, and processing special written requests.

UMS or Re-appear and Improvement Portal: Primary platform for applying for reappear and improvement examinations.

Dean or Examination Cell Staff: Point of contact for students seeking a special improvement chance for subjects not listed in the portal.
