TITLE LPU B.Tech Fee and Scholarship Structure (LPUNEST-Based) — Academic Session 2026

OVERVIEW This document outlines the fee structure and LPUNEST-based scholarship amounts applicable to students admitted to B.Tech and related programs at Lovely Professional University for the academic session 2026. Scholarship amounts are determined by the student's performance in LPUNEST, categorized under Cut-off bands A, B, C, and D. Different programs carry different fee structures and scholarship percentages.

KEY RULES AND POLICY DETAILS

General Conditions

Fee and scholarship details are applicable to students admitted on the basis of LPUNEST scores.

Scholarships are awarded as a percentage of the total program fee per semester, based on the LPUNEST cut-off band achieved by the student.

For B.Tech programs with the 2+2 International Credit Transfer Option, an additional International Transfer Fee of ₹50,000 is payable along with the first semester fee.

TITLE LPU Ph.D. Fee Structure and Scholarship Policy — Autumn Term 2026

OVERVIEW This document outlines the fee structure and available scholarships for students admitted to Ph.D. programs at Lovely Professional University for the Autumn Term 2026, on the basis of LPUNEST (Ph.D.). Scholarships are awarded based on LPUNEST scores, competitive examination qualifications, research outputs, and financial aid categories. The policy also details additional charges such as examination fee and thesis evaluation charges.

FEE STRUCTURE

The program fee for all Ph.D. programs (Full Time and Part Time) across all disciplines is ₹50,000 per semester.

This uniform fee applies to all available Ph.D. disciplines including Engineering, Sciences, Management, Humanities, Agriculture, Health Sciences, Design, Performing Arts, and Social Sciences.

Examination fee of ₹5,000 per semester is applicable separately. The examination fee for the first semester is payable along with the second semester fee or earlier.

Thesis evaluation charges of ₹20,000 are payable at the time of final Ph.D. thesis submission.

Central Instrumentation Facility (CIF) charges are applicable only if the services are used, and will be at subsidized rates.

No laboratory, experimentation, or term evaluation charges are applicable as a standard fee. If conducted separately for any individual candidate, charges will be as per actuals.

Program fee is applicable until the submission of the final thesis.

If a research scholar does not pay the semester fee as per the announced schedule, they may be de-registered from the program.

IMPORTANT CONDITIONS

All scholarship claims are subject to verification of valid scorecards, award letters, or research publications.

If a candidate fulfills JRF, NET, GATE, or GPAT requirements within 1.5 years of Ph.D. registration, scholarship eligibility under that category will apply from the immediately following term.

Scholarship for Ph.D. programs is applicable for a maximum of 4 years from the date of admission.

Research contingency for consumables is available for a maximum of 4 years from the date of admission.

If the program fee is reduced for any reason, the scholarship amount across all categories will be reduced proportionately. The fee payable after scholarship will remain unchanged.

The 20% financial aid for female candidates or LPU alumni is applicable only when no other scholarship is being availed.

Candidates claiming government, ministerial, or institutional fellowships must report to the university for original document verification, complete the linking process after eligibility confirmation, submit all original academic documents, opt for Coursework Option 1 (Saturday–Sunday), and comply with the rules of the concerned regulatory body.

In case of any dispute or ambiguity, the decision of the Head, Centre for Research Degree Programmes, shall be considered final.

CONTACT AND RESPONSIBLE OFFICE

Centre for Research Degree Programmes: Primary authority for Ph.D. admissions, scholarship verification, and policy interpretation.

Accounts or Finance Department: Responsible for fee payment schedules and processing of deductions.
