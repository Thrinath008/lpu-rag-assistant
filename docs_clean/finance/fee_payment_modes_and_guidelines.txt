TITLE LPU Fee Payment Modes, Schedules, and Guidelines

OVERVIEW Lovely Professional University provides students with multiple online and offline modes for paying academic, residential, and other facility charges. This document outlines the available payment methods, applicable convenience charges, EMI options, fee payment deadlines, and important conditions related to fee payment for all enrolled students.

KEY RULES AND POLICY DETAILS

Fee Payment Categories

Academic fees (including program fee, examination fee, uniform fee, and transport fee) and indoor facility charges must be paid to the respective Faculty Account.

Residential fee, mess fee, laundry fee, and gym fee must be paid to Pro Bricks Space Private Limited, as per the details mentioned in the fee deposit slip or relevant account number details.

Fee Payment Deadlines

First semester fee is payable at the time of admission.

Examination fee for the first semester is payable along with the second semester fee, or earlier.

Fees for successive semesters are payable before the end of teaching of the preceding semester.

Research scholars (Ph.D.) must pay semester fee as per the schedule announced by the university. Failure to do so may result in de-registration from the program.

Non-payment or delayed payment of fees may result in blocked registration for the next semester, ineligibility for examinations, or suspension of hostel or other facility access.

ONLINE PAYMENT OPTIONS

Option I — Net Banking, Debit Card, Credit Card, or UPI

This is the university's preferred payment method. All major cards and UPI platforms are accepted. Students are advised to increase their transaction limit if required before making payment.

Convenience charges vary by payment gateway and method. The following is a summary:

Zero Convenience Charge Methods:

UPI payments through all listed gateways (ICICI Easypay, PayTM, HDFC PayU, Jodo, Razorpay, Union Bank of India, CAMSPay, Cashfree): 0% for any amount.

RuPay Debit Card through ICICI Easypay, PayTM, Razorpay, CAMSPay, and Cashfree: 0% for any amount.

Net Banking through ICICI Easypay: 0% for any amount.

Standard Convenience Charges (indicative; subject to gateway):

Other Debit Cards: ₹13 flat to ₹25 + GST depending on gateway.

Credit Cards: Approximately 0.70% to 1.10% + GST depending on gateway.

International Credit Cards: 2.75% to 3.0% + GST depending on gateway.

Net Banking (non-ICICI): ₹8 to ₹15 + GST depending on gateway.

Recommended zero-cost payment: UPI or RuPay Debit Card through Paytm gateway.

OFFLINE PAYMENT OPTIONS

Option I — Cash Deposit at Bank into University Account

Students may deposit fees directly into the university's bank account.

After depositing, the fee deposit details or deposit slip must be communicated to the university through the Fee Intimation Slip or by emailing: admission.online@lpu.co.in (applicable for students admitted in 2025–26 or earlier batches).

Option II — Cash Deposit at University Campus

Fee may be paid in cash at the University Accounts Office on campus.

Option III — Demand Draft or Pay Order

A Demand Draft or Pay Order must be drawn in favor of the relevant Lovely Faculty in which the student has taken admission, payable at Jalandhar.

The Demand Draft or Pay Order must include the student's name and Candidate ID or Registration Number.

Example: For a B.Tech student, the Demand Draft must be in favor of "Lovely Faculty of Technology and Sciences," payable at Jalandhar.

It can be submitted in person at the university campus, at designated Camp Offices, or sent by post to: Lovely Professional University, Jalandhar–Delhi G.T. Road, Punjab, India — 144411.

ADDITIONAL FEE COMPONENTS

Examination Fee: ₹5,000 per semester. The first semester examination fee is payable along with the second semester fee or earlier.

Thesis Evaluation Fee (Ph.D.): ₹20,000, payable at the time of final thesis submission.

CIF Charges: Applicable only when the Central Instrumentation Facility is used, at subsidized rates.

The term "Program Fee" includes tuition and development fees, registration and enrollment fees, fees for cultural, sports, club, and co-curricular activities, and fees for research contingency, outreach, placement, medical, and counselling services.

IMPORTANT WARNINGS

No representative of Lovely Professional University will ask for transfer of funds to a personal account or engage in personal chats with students or guardians regarding fee payment.

Any such activity must be reported immediately to: grievance.doa@lpu.co.in.

CONTACT AND RESPONSIBLE OFFICE

University Accounts Office (On Campus): For cash payments and fee-related queries.

Fee Payment Portal: Available on the university website for online payments.

Email for Fee Intimation (2025–26 batch and earlier): admission.online@lpu.co.in

Grievance Reporting (Fraud or Impersonation): grievance.doa@lpu.co.in
