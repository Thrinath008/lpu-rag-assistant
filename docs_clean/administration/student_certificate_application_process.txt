TITLE LPU Student Certificate Application Process

OVERVIEW Students at Lovely Professional University can apply for official certificates through the university's online interface. This process covers the application for various certificates, profile verification, fee payment, and collection of certificates. Students are advised to read the complete instructions before initiating any application to ensure a smooth and timely process.

KEY RULES AND POLICY DETAILS

Profile Verification Before Applying

Before applying for any certificate, students must verify the accuracy of their personal credentials as displayed in the interface. This includes: Full Name, Gender, Father's Name, Mother's Name, and Date of Birth.

If any credential is incorrect or mismatched, the student must apply for rectification by clicking the "Profile Updation" tab and uploading a scanned copy of the 10th class mark sheet or DMC for authentication.

No certificate application can be processed while a profile updation request is pending.

Profile updation is completed within 3 working days from the date of the request.

Certificate Collection

After fee payment, the system will display the designated date of issue of the certificate under the "Certificate Issue Details" tab.

Students must collect their certificate on or before 30 days from the displayed date of issue.

If a student fails to collect the certificate within 30 days, the certificate will be destroyed by the university and the student must re-apply and pay the fee again. Certificate fees are non-refundable under any circumstances.

If the date of issue falls on a gazetted university holiday, the student may collect the certificate on the immediate next working day.

Token Number for Collection

At the time of applying, a token number is allocated to the student's request and is visible under the "Certificate Issue Details" tab.

The student must note this token number and present it to the concerned dealing official at the time of collecting the certificate.

Express Mode

Students who require a certificate urgently on the same day of application may apply through Express Mode.

Additional charges apply for Express Mode as per university policy.

PROCEDURE — STEP-BY-STEP APPLICATION PROCESS

Log in to the university's online interface and verify that all personal credentials are correct.

If any credentials are incorrect, click "Profile Updation," upload the required documents, and wait up to 3 working days for the update to be processed.

Once credentials are confirmed as correct, click "Apply Certificate."

Select the required certificate(s) from the available list and complete the fee payment.

After payment, note the date of issue displayed under the "Certificate Issue Details" tab.

Note the token number assigned to the application from the same tab.

Visit the designated collection window on or after the date of issue and present the token number to the dealing official.

If urgent certificate issuance is required on the same day, select Express Mode during the application and pay the applicable additional charges.

IMPORTANT CONDITIONS

Certificate fees are strictly non-refundable under any circumstances.

If a certificate is not collected within 30 days of the issue date, it will be destroyed and the student must re-apply and pay the full fee again.

No application for any certificate will be accepted while a profile updation request is under process.

For any queries or issues related to certificates, students should use the query or support link available within the certificate interface.
